About

DarkSky Beijing is a chapter of DarkSky International, dedicated to restoring the nighttime environment and protecting communities from the harmful effects of light pollution through outreach, advocacy, and conservation. Our mission is to raise awareness of the importance of dark skies, and to advocate for responsible outdoor lighting practices.

暗夜北京是 暗夜国际（DarkSky International，旧称国际暗夜协会IDA）的北京分会，致力于通过推广教育、公共宣传和环保行动来恢复夜间环境，并保护人居社区及野生动植物免受光污染的有害影响。我们的使命是增进公众对暗夜保护的重要性的认知，并倡导负责任的户外照明实践。
